About Us

Our mission is to transform ordinary Goa trips into extraordinary journeys of discovery. We aim to provide authentic and immersive experiences that connect travelers with the true essence of Goa—its vibrant nature, rich culture, thrilling adventures, and spirited lifestyle. By offering carefully curated experiences, we ensure that every traveler explores Goa beyond the usual tourist spots, discovering hidden gems and local traditions. Whether it's savoring Goan cuisine, engaging with local communities, or exploring untouched beaches and lush landscapes, we strive to create meaningful and memorable moments that reflect the heart and soul of Goa, making each trip truly special.

OUR STORY

Where Passion Meets Paradise

Safari Sutra Holidays began with a simple belief: that Goa is more than just beaches and parties—it's a complex tapestry of culture, adventure, tranquility, and natural wonder waiting to be experienced authentically.

Our journey began when a group of travel enthusiasts from diverse backgrounds found themselves repeatedly returning to Goa, each time discovering new facets of this coastal paradise that tourists rarely experience. We realized that while millions visit Goa each year, few truly connect with its soul—the hidden waterfalls accessible only by forest trails, ancient temples nestled in village communities, pristine beaches unmarked on tourist maps, and the rich Indo-Portuguese heritage that flavors every aspect of Goan life.

In 2014, Safari Sutra Holidays was born from this realization, and our signature brand "Chalo Goa" emerged as our promise to unveil the authentic Goa experience. The name "Safari Sutra" reflects our philosophy—"Safari" representing the journey of discovery, and "Sutra" embodying the thread that connects travelers to meaningful experiences.

What began as curated adventure tours for friends soon expanded into a comprehensive travel service as word spread about our unique approach. Today, we take pride in having helped thousands of travelers—from solo adventurers to honeymooners to multi-generational families—discover the Goa that locals cherish.

Why Choose Chalo Goa?

Local Expertise

Our team includes native Goans who share insider knowledge you won't find in guidebooks. We know which beaches have the best sunsets, which village kitchens serve the most authentic xacuti, and which trails lead to the most spectacular viewpoints.

Curated Experiences

We don't offer generic packages. Each experience is thoughtfully designed to showcase Goa's diversity—from adrenaline-pumping waterfall rappelling to meditative sunrise yoga sessions on secluded beaches.

Personalized Attention

With Chalo Goa, you're never just another booking. Our team takes the time to understand your preferences, creating customized itineraries that reflect your travel style.

Sustainable Practices

We were practicing eco-friendly tourism before it became trendy. Our trekking follows leave-no-trace principles, our beach camps prioritize zero waste, and our partnerships support conservation efforts across Goa.

Seamless Planning

From airport pickups to specialized dietary requirements, we handle the details so you can focus on the experience.

Community Connections

Through our network of local artisans, chefs, musicians, and storytellers, we offer authentic cultural exchanges that benefit both travelers and communities.
